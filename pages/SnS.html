Sweet and sour (serves 4)

A delicious homemade, crispy sweet and sour dish that rivals your favourite takeaway or store bought sauce

Ingredients

  • flour (enough to coat chicken)
  • 3 Chicken breasts (diced into bitesize pieces)
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1 large onion (peeled and chopped into large chunks)
  • 1 yellow pepper (chopped into large chunks)
  • 1 red pepper (chopped into large chunks)
  • 2 cloves of garlic (peeled and chopped/crushed)
  • 1 teaspoon of minced ginger
  • 150ml tomato ketchup
  • 2 tablespoons malt vinegar
  • 6 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 475g (tin) pinapple chunks in juice
  • rice

Method

  1. coat the diced chicken in the flour and shallow fry or deep fry until golden then remove and place into a bowl lined with kitchen towel (best to do this in batches so the chicken doesnt clump together)
  2. cook rice as per instructions on pack
  3. whilst chicken is cooking start the sauce. heat a little bit of oil in a large frying pan or wok on a medium high heat, add the onions and cook for 3 minutes stirring every now and then untill the onions start to turn translucent.
  4. add the peppers and cook for a further minute
  5. add add the garlic and ginger and cook for another minute
  6. add tomato ketchup, vinegar, brown sugar, pineapple (including the juice) and stir. bring to the boil then turn down the heat and let it bubble stirring occasionally until thickened
  7. transfer chicken to the sauce and mix to coat
  8. serve with rice and enjoy
