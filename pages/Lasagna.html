Lasagna

An absolute family classic. a quick and easy meal that will gather everyone around the table

Ingredients

  • 500g beef mince
  • 2 tins of chopped tomatoes
  • 1 large onion diced
  • 2 cloves of garlic chopped/crushed
  • 1 tablespoon of tomato puree
  • mixed herbs
  • basil
  • 1 pack of dried lasagna sheets
  • 1 jar white lasagna sauce
  • grated cheese of choice
  • garlic baguette
  • bistro salad

Method

  1. pre-heat oven to 200 degreess celcius
  2. in a large pan brown the mince with a sprinkle of salt and the onions on a medium high heat stirring occasionally
  3. once onions start to go translucent and mince is browned (roughly 4/5 mins) add the chopped tomatoes and stir through
  4. stir in the tomato puree and garlic and cook for 1 minute bring to the boil then turn down to a simmer
  5. ad the mixed herbs and basil to taste and simmer until thickened
  6. once thickened build the layers of the lasagna (bolognese, lasagna sheet, white sauce) to the required height in an ovenproof dish (make sure top layer is the white sauce) TIP:use the back of a serving spoon or a spare sheet of pasta to spread sauces evenly
  7. place on a middle shelf of the oven for 15 minutes
  8. cook garlic baguette as per instructions on pack
  9. sprinkle grated cheese on lasagna and return to oven for 5 minutes or until cheese is melted
  10. serve lasagna with garlic baguette and bistro salad and enjoy
